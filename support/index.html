Quick answers

How do I start a game?

Tap Create Room, pick a game, and share the short room code with friends. They tap Join Room and enter the code — that's it.

My camera or microphone isn't working

Make sure you granted PlayParty camera & microphone permission in your device settings, and that no other app is using the camera. You can toggle your mic/camera from the video bar at any time.

Is PlayParty free?

Yes — every game is free to play.
