About PlayParty

Party games you actually play together — on live video, wherever your friends are.

What PlayParty is

PlayParty is a multiplayer party-games app for phones and the web. One person creates a room, picks a game, and shares a short room code; everyone else joins from their own device. What makes it different from a regular quiz or board-game app is that you all share a live video and voice call while you play — so the bluffing, the reactions, the laughing and the "wait, that was YOU?" moments happen face to face, even when you're miles apart.

It's built for game nights, long-distance friends, family catch-ups and quick breaks — anything from two people to a full room of twenty. Every game is free to play.

The games

PlayParty is a growing collection of quick social-deduction and reflex games, including Imposter, Corp & Theft, Abracadabra, Who Better Lie and Math Rapid Fire. Each has its own simple rules but the same idea: easy to learn in a minute, more fun the better you know the people you're playing with. See the full list and how-to-play guides on the Games page.

Who makes it

PlayParty is built and operated by Imautotech, a small independent studio. We're focused on real-time social experiences that bring people together rather than keep them scrolling alone. We add new games regularly and listen closely to player feedback.

Privacy & safety

Live video and voice are never recorded or stored — they exist only for the duration of your call. You control your camera and microphone at all times. You can read the full details in our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service, and you can delete your account and data any time from the account-deletion page.

Questions, bug reports, partnership ideas or feedback are always welcome — head to our Contact page or the Help Center.
