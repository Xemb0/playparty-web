The games

Four games. Endless chaos.

Short rounds, big reactions. Jump between them without leaving the room.

4–10 players

Imposter

Everyone gets the secret word — except the impostor. Drop clues, sniff out the faker, and vote them out before they blend in for the win.

2–10 players

Guess It

Describe it, draw it, or act it out on camera. First to shout the secret word scores — fastest fingers and wildest charades win.

2–8 players

Lucky Dice

Roll, bank, or push your luck for one more throw. Race to the target score — but greed busts your turn. Pure tabletop tension.

2–6 players

Collector

Flip boxes on a grid to collect flowers, sheep and lions. Predators eat prey in your stash — biggest food chain wins. Watch for bombs.

How it works

Three taps to chaos.

1

Create a room

Hit Create Room and you get a private room with a shareable link and code — no setup, no accounts needed to start.

2

They tap the link and hop in from any device with camera & mic. See everyone live, react in real time, talk smack.

3

Pick a game & play

Choose any of the four games, play a quick round, and rematch or swap games instantly — all without leaving the call.
